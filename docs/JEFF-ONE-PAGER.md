## The problem we're fixing

Tideline currently spends an estimated **70 hours/month** managing pay applications across active FAU projects. That's nearly half of a full-time admin's job, on a process that universities reject roughly 30-40% of the time on first submission. Every kick-back delays cash collection by 7-14 days.

**That's not a software gap. That's a margin gap.**

---

## What Pay App Pro does

Built specifically for Florida public university work — not generic G702/G703.

- **AIA G702/G703 forms** with university-specific extras: certified payroll attached automatically when Davis-Bacon applies, BOT approval gates on COs, retainage auto-reduction at 50% per F.S. 255.078, mixed-funding splits for state + auxiliary + donor projects
- **Sub billing portal** — subs submit through their own login, you approve or kick back, system rolls their billing into your pay app automatically
- **Auto-rolled work-completed-previous columns** — no more spreadsheet drift, no more "wait, what did we bill last month?"
- **Pre-submission validation** — the system catches the things universities kick back for *before* you hit submit
- **Audit trail** — every submission, approval, and dollar tracked with timestamps
